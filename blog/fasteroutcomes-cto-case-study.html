Executive Summary

FasterOutcomes is an AI-powered legal tech platform that helps legal teams automate document analysis and workflow management. As Fractional CTO, I built the entire technical foundation from scratch in 12 weeks—from greenfield to production-ready MVP.

  • ✅ 12 weeks from concept to production MVP

The Context

FasterOutcomes is an early-stage legal tech startup tackling a major pain point: legal teams spend countless hours manually analyzing documents and managing complex workflows. The founders had domain expertise and a clear vision but needed a technical co-founder to build the platform.

The Opportunity

  • Strong product-market fit signals: 10+ potential customers identified, all experiencing the same pain
  • Clear value proposition: Reduce document review time by 70%+
  • AI-first approach: Perfect timing as LLM capabilities matured
  • Experienced founders: 15+ years in legal industry

The Challenge

  • Greenfield project: No existing codebase
  • Ambitious timeline: 12 weeks to production MVP
  • Limited budget: Seed-stage constraints ($500K raised)
  • Complex domain: Legal compliance, privacy, accuracy requirements
  • Part-time CTO: 10-15 hrs/week initially, scaling to 20-30 hrs/week

Tech Stack Decisions

1. Firebase for Backend

Why: Fast development, built-in auth, real-time updates, managed infrastructure

Trade-off: Vendor lock-in, but acceptable for MVP speed

Result: Saved 4-6 weeks of backend development time

2. LangGraph over LangChain Alone

Why: Complex multi-step workflows with state management needed

Benefit: Clean separation of workflow logic, easier debugging

Result: Maintainable AI pipeline that non-technical team can understand

3. Serverless Architecture (Cloud Functions + Cloud Run)

Why: Cost-effective for early-stage, scales automatically

Cost: $500/month vs $5K+ for always-on servers

Result: 90% infrastructure cost savings while maintaining production quality

4. React + TypeScript for Frontend

Why: Fast development, strong typing prevents bugs, great ecosystem

Framework: Chose React over Vue/Angular for hiring pool and libraries

Result: Maintainable codebase for future team growth

Lessons for Founders

1. Fractional CTO Works

10-15 hrs/week can build production systems. Key is focused work on high-leverage tasks: architecture decisions, critical code, team mentorship. This model works when founders handle product/business and CTO handles technical execution.

2. Managed Services Accelerate MVPs

Firebase, Vercel, Stripe, Pinecone saved months of development. Yes, there's vendor lock-in. But for early-stage startups, speed > flexibility. You can always migrate later if needed (we haven't needed to).

3. LangChain Ecosystem is Production-Ready

Many dismiss LangChain as "just for POCs." Wrong. With proper architecture (LangGraph for state management, proper error handling, monitoring), it's production-grade. We process thousands of documents monthly with 99.9% uptime.

4. Equity + Cash is Win-Win

1% equity + $8K/month aligns incentives. I'm invested in long-term success (equity) while being compensated fairly for current work (cash). This model works better than equity-only or cash-only for fractional roles.

5. Ship Fast, Iterate Based on Real Usage

We launched with 60% of planned features. First customer feedback shaped remaining 40%. This beat the alternative: spending 6 months building features nobody wanted. Real usage data > assumptions.
